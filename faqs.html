Sessions

  • How long is each session?

    Sessions last 50 minutes.

  • What can I expect from the first few sessions?

    During the first sessions, you can expect an intake and an exploration of your goals. During the second session, I will introduce EMDR. During the third session, I will introduce IFS, and after that we will use EMDR and IFS together, relying on one, the other or both depending on the need.

Availability

  • When are you available?

    I offer therapy sessions online via video only. I'm available most weekdays during the day. I don't work on Fridays.

  • How can I know if you are a good much for me?
